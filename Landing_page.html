Our History

Launched in 2024, Insights & Solutions Consulting leverages the extensive experience of its founder and managing director, Lisa McGregor. With 30+ years of experience working with USAID, RTI International, CARE International, the International Rescue Committee and the United Nations Development Program, Lisa has served in longer-term positions in six countries and provided short term support in more than 20 countries and communities. Her expertise includes team leadership, technical assessments, applied political economy analysis, integrated governance across sectors, local organizational capacity development, strategic planning and meeting facilitation, and other technical approaches.

At Insights & Solutions Consulting, we are committed to sustainable development. Our approach combines local insights with practical yet innovative solutions for communities and individuals while ensuring our clients achieve their development goals. Our deep experience and dedication to excellence make us a trusted partner in the international development field.

Meet the Team

Lisa McGregor

Founder and Managing Director

Lisa McGregor is the Founder and Managing Director of Insights & Solutions Consulting where she leverages her extensive expertise in international development, integrated governance across sectors, applied political economy analysis (PEA), strategic planning and meeting facilitation, and related technical assessments. Lisa has supported technical teams to enhance outcomes in governance; gender equity and social inclusion; natural resource management; agriculture; water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH); health systems; public financial management and other areas.

Her career includes long-term technical and team management roles in Sudan, Bangladesh, Thailand, Bangladesh, Haiti, and East Africa/Kenya and short-term assistance in 20+ countries with USAID, RTI International, CARE International, the International Rescue Committee, and the United Nations Development Program. She is a native English speaker with proficiency in spoken and written French and basic spoken Arabic. (She also spoke proficient Bangla and Thai while working in Bangladesh and Thailand.)
