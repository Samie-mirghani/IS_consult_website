What We Do

We partner with mission-driven organizations to help them design, implement, and evaluate initiatives that advance democracy, human rights, and inclusive development. Our approach is collaborative, grounded in evidence, and tailored to local contexts.

Strategy & Advisory

We help organizations navigate complexity through data-informed strategy, contextual analysis, and tailored support across the project lifecycle.

Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning

Our MEL services strengthen accountability and learning. We design and implement practical, participatory systems for measuring outcomes and impact.

Capacity Strengthening

We work side-by-side with local actors to build sustainable capacity in program design, adaptive management, and evidence use.

Applied Research

From political economy analysis to surveys and case studies, our research generates actionable insights for policy and practice.

Facilitation & Co-Creation

We design and lead inclusive workshops and co-creation sessions that align stakeholders, spark innovation, and build shared ownership.

Organizational Learning

We support teams to reflect, learn, and adapt through after-action reviews, learning agendas, and evidence-to-practice initiatives.
